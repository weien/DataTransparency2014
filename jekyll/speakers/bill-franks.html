--- layout: default title: Bill Franks ---

Bill Franks is Chief Analytics Officer for Teradata, where he provides insight on trends in the analytics & big data space and helps clients understand how Teradata and its analytic partners can support their efforts. In addition, Franks is a faculty member of the International Institute for Analytics and the author of the book Taming The Big Data Tidal Wave. His second book, The Analytics Revolution, is coming in Fall 2014. He is also a sought after speaker and frequent blogger. His work has spanned clients in a variety of industries for companies ranging in size from Fortune 100 companies to small non-profit organizations.
