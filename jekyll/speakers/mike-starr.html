Before joining Workiva, Mike served as Deputy Chief Accountant for policy in the Office of the Chief Accountant of the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission where he focused on understanding investorsâ€™ financial information needs and the role of structured data in meeting those needs. He previously served as the Chief Operating Officer for Grant Thornton International Ltd., where he played a key role in implementing the global strategy and oversaw public policy and other critical functions.
