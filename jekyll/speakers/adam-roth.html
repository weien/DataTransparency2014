--- layout: default title: Adam Roth ---

Adam Roth was Chief Operating Officer for West Side Ecumenical Ministry (WSEM), before serving as President of StreamLink Software. As COO, Adam supervised all of WSEM’s programs and was responsible for system development and integration, quality control, resource development, new program initiatives and community collaborations. Adam also served as interim CEO during a leadership change in 2004. While COO, Adam monitored an operating budget of $12 million, more than double WSEM’s operating budget when Adam started at WSEM as the Director of Operations in March 1998.
