Amy Edwards is the Director of the Government Performance Task Force U.S. Senate Budget Committee. The Senate Budget Committee's Government Performance Task Force was established in 2009 to examine the information base for decision making and identify opportunities to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of federal programs and services. As the Director of the Task Force, Edwards supports Senator Mark R. Warner (D-VA) as the Chairman. Prior to her current post, she was the Manager of the Government Performance Project at the Pew Center on the States, where she produced the 50-state management report card.
